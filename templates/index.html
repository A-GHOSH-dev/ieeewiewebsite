About Us

Empowering Women In Engineering.

IEEE Women in Engineering is an organization dedicated to uplifting women engineers and scientists.

Our main goal is to provide a support system for women through our professional endeavors and inspire each other to follow our academic interests and goals. Here at IEEE-WIE, we elevate, motivate, and educate our fellow sisters to achieve our ambitions and dreams!

Our Events

IEEE WIE conducts various technical events and non-technical events throughout the year. Take a look at some of our events.

WeHACK

School of Electrical Engineering in collaboration with IEEE-WIE organized WeHack 3.0 in coordination with Honeywell, Bangalore as a part of their Honeywell Automation Challenge 2019 which had cash prizes worth of lakhs and hundreds of participants.

Meme-o-mania

Memes and Gifs seem to have taken over the internet, so much so that they seem to have become a part of our daily life. Our event meme-o-mania was aimed as bringing out the witty humor of the millennials and awarding the best for their creativity.

Code Run Seek

A event loved by all, Code Run Seek is an event that IEEE WIE has conducted in collaboration with IEEE IAS for Gravitas multiple times. The event is unique because not only do you have to code but you also have to decode our clues and earn your problem statement!

CodeZers

Coding and hacking is not just a hobby, it is a profession. It is another successful gravitas event of ours which focuses to test and develop the participants coding skills while providing a competitive environment

Unique Ad

Lights, camera, action! Our Riviera event Unique ad was focused on bringing out the director in the pariticipants by asking them to put together a short advertisement on a given topic. The event was in collaboration with IEEE-IAS.

Gem Jam

The event provided all the tech geeks a platform to express their ideas on trending technology. The catch here, is that you just have a minute to win it! The best speaker was given a cash prize.

Our Gravitas Events

Meme o Mania

The virtual event is a meme creating event consisting of 3 rounds in total , participants will be divided into teams and will have to work . The teams creativity and based on the norms they will be going on to the next level. Round 1 –Participants will be creating a GIF in 1 hour. Round 2- Participants should create a meme on the Gravitas theme in 30 mins. Round 3 – The memes will be posted on the social media platforms and the winners will be declared based on the no. of likes. There are 2 final winning teams, 1st place and 2nd place.

Forge e Logo

Graphic designing is the growing field. Forge-e-Logo will help you start in the most demanding area i.e. Logo designing. Many new startups are budding up among youngsters and a good logo is key to a successful branding. It will be one day online event of two rounds. For Round 1, different hypothetical fictional brands and their requirements will be given to participants for designing. For Round 2, common problem statement will be given. The designs will be judged according to the novelty and creativity of designs. Internship and prize money will be awarded accordingly.

Our Goals

Empowered Women Empowers Women

Our goal at IEEE WIE VIT is to invision a society where men and women alike work together to bring about innovations that help in in technological fields. We aim to -

Recognize the talents and achievements of women in every field

Enhance leadership skills in women so that they are ready to become leaders of tomorrow

Organize workshops and technical conferences to enhance networking and to promote membership in IEEE WIE.

About IEEE WIE

IEEE WIE is one of the world’s leaders in changing the face of engineering. Our global network connects nearly 20,000 members in over 100 countries to advance women in technology at all points in their life and career. IEEE WIE members make lifelong friendships, acquire influential mentors, and make a difference for the benefit of humanity.
