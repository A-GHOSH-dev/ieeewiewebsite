Glossary

The virtual event is a meme creating event consisting of 3 rounds in total , participants will be divided into teams and will have to work . The teams creativity and based on the norms they will be going on to the next level. Round 1 –Participants will be creating a GIF in 1 hour. Round 2- Participants should create a meme on the Gravitas theme in 30 mins. Round 3 – The memes will be posted on the social media platforms and the winners will be declared based on the no. of likes. There are 2 final winning teams, 1st place and 2nd place.



Headlines

Many new startups are budding up among youngsters and a good logo is key to a successful branding. It will be one day online event of two rounds.
